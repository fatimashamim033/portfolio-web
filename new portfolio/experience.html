About APT ACADEMY & OBJECTIVES

Welcome to APT Academy, where education knows no bounds! With a team of five committed teachers, we cater to a diverse range of students, from Class 1 to college level. Our aim extends beyond tutoring; we aspire to evolve into a full-fledged school, offering a comprehensive educational journey. Additionally, we aim to introduce computer classes, providing holistic and modern learning experiences for our students.

Extracurricular Activities

We know the importance of extracurricular activities in shaping a well-rounded education, so Apt Academy provides a variety of activities. For example:

  • Mefil-e-Melad: A cultural event with decorations, rituals, and community involvement.
  • Drood Khuwani: A significant activity held regularly, with enthusiastic student participation.
  • Gaming Day: A day dedicated to games and activities that fosters teamwork and community.
  • Planting: Promotes environmental consciousness with regular planting activities.
